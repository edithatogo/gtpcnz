# Launch post red-team controls v1.7.2

Each public post should pass these controls before publication:

1. Does it treat current reform as the comparator rather than a straw man?
2. Does it avoid claiming calibrated prediction or fiscal savings?
3. If it says “uncapped”, does it immediately say scheduled, rules-based, audited and place-accountable?
4. Does it avoid “abolish PHOs” and instead separate PHO functions from payment intermediation/pass-through?
5. Does it say equity protections are core, not optional?
6. Does it avoid implying Accident Compensation Corporation can be copied wholesale?
7. Does it avoid implying hospital growth has only one cause?
8. Does it include a “what would change my mind” section?
9. Does the figure caption say whether the figure is conceptual, model-generated or observed data?
10. Does it include an appendix link for readers who want evidence/detail?

## Gate
Posts 7–18 should not be finalised until the first-six-post feedback gate, equity review, and early stakeholder validation are considered.
