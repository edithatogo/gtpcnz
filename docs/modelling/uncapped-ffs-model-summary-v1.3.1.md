# Uncapped FFS model summary — v1.3.1

This demonstrative model tests the clarified policy proposition: remove the global cap on eligible primary medical activity and introduce an ACC-like fee-for-service stream, while retaining capitation and place-based accountability. It is source-informed and conceptual, not predictive.

## Interpretation

The model distinguishes two uncapping pathways. An uncapped medical FFS stream **without** place accountability and strong controls improves marginal supply but raises gaming and equity/fiscal risk. An uncapped medical FFS stream **with** capitation, place accountability, co-payment protections and audit performs substantially better.
