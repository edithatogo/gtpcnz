# Calibration data register

## New Zealand public data candidates

- Ministry of Health capitation reweighting documents.
- Sapere capitation reweighting report and briefing.
- National Enrolment Service data, subject to access.
- National Primary Care Dataset, once operational/reporting.
- Health NZ health targets.
- ED presentations and hospitalisation datasets.
- Ambulatory sensitive hospitalisation indicators.
- Health NZ ambulance performance reports.
- ACC claims data.
- PHO financial statements and Ministry PHO finance briefing.
- NZ Health Survey access and unmet need measures.
- Health workforce data.
- Stats NZ population projections.

## Australia public data candidates

- MBS item data.
- PBS data.
- AIHW primary care indicators.
- AIHW ED and potentially preventable hospitalisation data.
- NHRA hospital funding and activity data.
- PHN performance data.
- Australian GP Incentives Review.
- ABS population and rurality data.

## Data access notes

Data access will determine whether modelling remains exploratory/synthetic or becomes empirically calibrated.
