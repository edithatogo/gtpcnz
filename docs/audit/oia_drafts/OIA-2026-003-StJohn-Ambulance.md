# Official Information Act Request: OIA-2026-003
**To:** St John Ambulance / Wellington Free Ambulance
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Subject:** Ambulance Disposition and Non-conveyance Rates

Dear Official Information Team,

Under the Official Information Act 1982, I request the following aggregated information regarding ambulance dispatch and patient dispositions over the last 24 months:

1. **Disposition Rates:** The monthly proportion of incidents resulting in "Treat and Refer" or "Hear and Treat" (non-conveyance) versus "Conveyance to Emergency Department".
2. **Re-presentation Risk:** Any data or internal evaluation showing the rate at which patients managed via alternative pathways (non-conveyance) re-present to an Emergency Department or require a secondary ambulance call-out within 48 hours.
3. **Regional Breakdown:** If possible, please provide the data broken down by region or District Health Board (former DHB) boundaries.

I prefer to receive the information in a machine-readable electronic format (e.g., CSV or Excel).
