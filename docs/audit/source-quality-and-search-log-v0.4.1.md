# Source quality and search log — v0.4.1

## 1. Source hierarchy

Use sources in the following order of preference:

1. Official NZ Government documents: Ministry of Health, Health NZ, Treasury, ACC, Cabinet papers, Waitangi Tribunal.
2. Official Australian Government documents: Department of Health and Aged Care, AIHW, NHRA documents, Productivity Commission, Treasury.
3. Peer-reviewed evidence: systematic reviews, Cochrane reviews, experimental economics, health economics, simulation modelling, primary care workforce/access studies.
4. Professional college and sector documents: RACGP, RNZCGP, ACRRM, RACMA, GPNZ, GenPro, ambulance sector material.
5. Grey literature and journalism: useful for leads, not primary support unless corroborated.

## 2. Reporting standards to apply later

- PRISMA-ScR for scoping-review reporting.
- PRISMA 2020 if a full systematic review is undertaken.
- STRESS for empirical simulation reporting using system dynamics, agent-based or discrete-event approaches.
- ODD for agent-based model description.
- CHEERS 2022 if a formal economic evaluation is produced.
