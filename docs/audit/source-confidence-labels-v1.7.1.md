# Source-confidence labels v1.7.1

Use these labels in reports, Substack appendices and stakeholder material to avoid blurring evidence, theory, assumptions and judgement.

- **Public document:** Directly supported by official/public material; cite and link the source.
- **Peer-reviewed evidence:** Supported by journal article, systematic review or recognised academic source.
- **Economic theory:** Supported by standard microeconomic, game-theoretic or transaction-cost logic.
- **Model assumption:** Used in the scaffold and needs empirical validation.
- **Stakeholder intelligence:** Raised by email/interview/stakeholder material but not independently verified.
- **Policy judgement:** Normative recommendation or judgement about architecture, risk or sequencing.
