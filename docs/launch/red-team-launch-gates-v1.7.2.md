# Red-team launch gates v1.7.2

Do not publish the next stage unless these gates are satisfied.

## Gate 1 — Wording
- Every use of "uncapped" is paired with scheduled, rules-based, audited, clinically governed or place-accountable language.
- No post suggests uncapped prices or uncontrolled billing.

## Gate 2 — Current reform comparator
- Current reforms are acknowledged as meaningful.
- The critique is framed as "may be insufficient", not "nothing is happening".

## Gate 3 — Equity
- Co-payment and fee-for-service equity risks are named.
- Māori/Pacific/equity review is required before recommendation-heavy posts.

## Gate 4 — Model limits
- Model figures say they are scenario indices, not observed outcomes.
- No post claims a quantified hospital or fiscal effect.

## Gate 5 — PHO language
- PHOs are discussed as functions versus intermediation, pass-through, transparency and non-capitated funding.
- No blanket abolition language.

## Gate 6 — RACMA
- No active RACMA sponsorship request.
- If RACMA re-engages, send the decision-maker summary and model card only.

## Gate 7 — Evidence status
- Stakeholder intelligence and internal-document references remain labelled as OIA targets, not public evidence.
