# RACMA exclusion note v1.7.1

This release removes RACMA from the active engagement pathway.

## Rationale
Feedback in the email trail suggested that the topic may not sit naturally within RACMA's current policy and advocacy priorities, and that a peer-reviewed, primary-care-sector or public-facing route may be more appropriate.

## Current position
- Do not proactively ask RACMA to sponsor, endorse or own the work.
- Do not frame the next step as a RACMA policy paper.
- If RACMA comes back interested, provide a short decision-maker summary, model card and caveated discussion note.
- Keep historical RACMA references in archived files where they describe the development pathway, but do not use RACMA as the current publication strategy.

## Preferred current pathway
Substack public education, OIA/data requests, equity review, stakeholder validation, policy brief refinement, and possible academic Viewpoint/protocol publication.
