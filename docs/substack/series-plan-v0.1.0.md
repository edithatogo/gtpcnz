# Substack series plan: The Primary Care Funding Game

## Series purpose

Build public understanding before making the policy ask. The series should make readers comfortable with the idea that funding models are not neutral: they shape supply, access, professional behaviour, market entry, patient choices and hospital demand.

## Tone

Plain-English, analytical, mildly provocative, non-partisan.

## Publication cadence

One post per week for 12 weeks. Release posts 1–4 before any formal policy brief, so the audience understands the concepts first.

## Recurring line

> Stop tightly rationing primary care and ambulance activity, then wondering why hospitals grow.

## Risk management

- Avoid implying all PHOs are bad.
- Avoid implying co-payments are harmless.
- Avoid implying pharmacists/NPs/paramedics can replace GPs in all circumstances.
- Avoid overclaiming hospital avoidance without modelling.
