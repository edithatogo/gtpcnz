# Source notes v0.5.0

This version adds a formal source registry and claim-to-source ledger. Key public source categories are:

1. **NZ capitation and reweighting** — Ministry capitation reweighting page and H2024057558 Sapere briefing.
2. **PHO accountability and transparency** — H2025069314 PHO finance briefing.
3. **Tier 1 and PHO contracting precedent** — 2020 Health and Disability System Review.
4. **Primary care access measurement** — Health NZ National Primary Care Dataset and 80% within-seven-days target material.
5. **Ambulance/prehospital reporting** — Health NZ Ambulance Team and performance-report material.
6. **Australia comparator** — Australian Review of General Practice Incentives.
7. **Reporting standards** — PRISMA-ScR, STRESS, ODD and CHEERS.

Important limitation: the capitation model is publicly described, but the full operational formula, live rate tables, pass-through arrangements and calculation workbook are not yet included in this repo. OIA001 is designed to address that gap.
