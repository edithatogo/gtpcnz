## What changed since v1.6.0

Hospital salience remains central, but the validation status is deliberately narrow. The v1.8.1 model can support aggregate plausibility about upstream architecture; it cannot claim measured ED or hospital-demand reduction.

## Insert/update guidance

Add this beside any hospital-facing examples. Keep hospital pressure as a policy concern, not as an estimated model outcome.

## Claim boundary

No ED reduction, hospital-demand reduction, or fiscal-savings claim should be added from v1.8.1. The release remains not valid for those impact claims.
