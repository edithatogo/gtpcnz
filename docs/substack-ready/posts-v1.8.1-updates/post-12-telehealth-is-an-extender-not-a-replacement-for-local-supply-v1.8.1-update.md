# v1.8.1 update note: Telehealth is an extender

Original post: `docs/substack-ready/posts-v1.6.0-public/post-12-telehealth-is-an-extender-not-a-replacement-for-local-supply-v1.6.0.md`

This is an additive update for the original post. It does not replace the original version.

## What changed since v1.6.0

Telehealth remains modelled as an extender rather than a replacement for local supply. The public aggregate validation lane does not validate a delivery-mode effect.

## Insert/update guidance

Add this after the central telehealth argument. Preserve the original distinction between access extension and local supply substitution.

## Claim boundary

Do not claim telehealth reduces demand, replaces local workforce, or resolves rural access constraints. Those are implementation claims outside the v1.8.1 evidence status.
