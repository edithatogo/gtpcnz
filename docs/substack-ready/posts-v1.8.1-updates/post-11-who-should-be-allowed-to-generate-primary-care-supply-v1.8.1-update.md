# v1.8.1 update note: Who should be allowed to generate primary care supply?

Original post: `docs/substack-ready/posts-v1.6.0-public/post-11-who-should-be-allowed-to-generate-primary-care-supply-v1.6.0.md`

This is an additive update for the original post. It does not replace the original version.

## What changed since v1.6.0

The v1.8.1 model sharpens the supply-generation question but does not validate workforce effects. Workforce remains a key uncertainty and evidence-priority domain.

## Insert/update guidance

Add this beside the discussion of who can generate supply. The update should frame workforce as a constraint to be tested, not as a measured output.

## Claim boundary

Do not claim workforce expansion, participation shifts, or scope-of-practice impacts. The model is not valid for workforce-effect estimates.
