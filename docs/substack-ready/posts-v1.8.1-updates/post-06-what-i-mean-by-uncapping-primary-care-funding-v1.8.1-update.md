# v1.8.1 update note: What I mean by uncapping primary care funding

Original post: `docs/substack-ready/posts-v1.6.0-public/post-06-what-i-mean-by-uncapping-primary-care-funding-v1.6.0.md`

This is an additive update for the original post. It does not replace the original version.

## What changed since v1.6.0

The v1.8.1 evidence lane reinforces that "uncapping" must mean scheduled, audited, place-accountable, and claim-gated funding. It is not an open-ended spending claim.

## Insert/update guidance

Add a caution box after the definition of uncapping. Make clear that the public aggregate validation supports testing a controlled architecture, not removing fiscal discipline.

## Claim boundary

Do not claim uncapping will produce precise savings, hospital deflection, or workforce effects. The supported claim is about the plausibility of a bounded architecture for public testing.
