# Draft OIA request: PHO roles, functions and options for change

To: Ministry of Health

Dear Sir/Madam,

I request under the Official Information Act 1982 the following information:

1. The briefing/advice referred to in Ministry material as **H2025067082**, described as advice on PHOs' roles, functions and options for change.
2. Any associated aide-mémoires, Cabinet papers, draft papers, appendices, emails, slides or implementation advice related to H2025067082.
3. Any advice since 1 January 2024 on whether PHOs should remain the gateway for public primary care funding.
4. Any advice since 1 January 2024 on direct payment to primary care providers, alternative commissioning arrangements, or national claims/payment platforms for primary care.
5. Any analysis of PHO transaction costs, financial transparency, ownership structures, pass-through requirements, conflicts of interest or barriers to provider market entry.

If any material is withheld, please identify the specific document or component withheld and the grounds for withholding.

Yours sincerely,

Dylan A Mordaunt
