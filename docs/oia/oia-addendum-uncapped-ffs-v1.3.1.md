# OIA addendum — uncapped FFS entitlement stream — v1.3.1

## Purpose

Request material needed to test whether New Zealand could migrate part of primary care funding to an ACC-like fee-for-service medical benefit stream.

## Ministry of Health / Health New Zealand requests

1. Any advice comparing capped capitation/programme funding with uncapped or demand-led benefit-style primary care payments.
2. Any fiscal analysis of allowing eligible primary care contacts to generate scheduled public contributions outside a fixed annual programme pool.
3. Any modelling of marginal supply response to fee-for-service, activity-based payments or access payments in primary care.
4. Any advice on using co-payment settings to calibrate use while protecting equity.
5. Any analysis of medical, nurse practitioner, nurse, pharmacist or allied health claimable item design for primary care.
6. Any advice on anti-gaming, audit, documentation, service-frequency rules, prior approval or claims analytics for primary care benefits.
7. Any documents comparing ACC Cost of Treatment Regulations, Medicare Benefits Schedule-style payments or other claims-based payment systems to NZ primary care funding.

## ACC / MBIE requests

1. Analyses of Cost of Treatment Regulations as a treatment contribution system, including provider behaviour, co-payments, frequency/duration controls and audit.
2. Any assessment of provider choice between regulations, contracts and purchase orders.
3. Any material on how ACC manages clinical necessity, scope, appropriate duration/frequency and billing compliance.
4. Any cross-system analysis of whether ACC activity-based treatment funding supports primary, urgent or allied health provider viability.
