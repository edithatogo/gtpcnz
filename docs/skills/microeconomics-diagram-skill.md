# Microeconomics diagram skill

Use this skill for general-audience diagrams about marginal cost, marginal payment, capitation, co-payments, demand, supply or gaming incentives.

## Rules

- Explain the mechanism, not a calibrated estimate.
- Keep axes plain: "extra care delivered", "payment for extra care", "patient price", "access".
- Avoid unexplained economics shorthand.
- Use one mechanism per figure.
- Add a caveat: "Illustrative economics diagram, not an estimate."

## Standard diagrams

- Marginal payment gap: scheduled payment versus marginal cost.
- Capitation constraint: fixed base funding versus rising care need.
- Co-payment barrier: higher patient price reducing access for price-sensitive groups.
- Controls frontier: activity-sensitive payment raising supply and audit/place accountability reducing gaming risk.
- Who bears the cost: government, practice and patient under funding designs.
