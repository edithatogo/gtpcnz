# GTPCNZ product context

GTPCNZ is a public policy-research and translation package on primary care funding architecture in Aotearoa New Zealand and Australia.

The public model/dashboard layer must be safe, clear and transparent:

- source-informed scaffold, not calibrated forecast;
- current reform pathway as comparator;
- uncapped does not mean uncontrolled;
- capitation plus scheduled fee-for-service plus place accountability;
- no Substack work in the model/dashboard hardening track.
