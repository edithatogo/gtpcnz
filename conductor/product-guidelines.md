# Product guidelines

## Public claim discipline

Use source-confidence labels and caveats. Do not claim precise savings, ED reductions, admissions reductions, workforce effects or government endorsement.

## Dashboard language

Use "explainer", "source-informed scaffold", "reference scenario", and "toy explainer". Avoid "forecast", "predict", "savings estimate" and "calibrated simulator" unless explicitly negated.

## Substack boundary

Substack is handled separately and is out of scope for Track 026.
