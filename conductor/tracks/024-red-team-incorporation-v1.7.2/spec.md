# Track 024 — Red-team incorporation v1.7.2

## Objective
Close the loop between critique and artefacts. The red-team and devil's advocate reviews should not sit as separate documents; they should visibly change the publishable thesis, model caveats, Substack launch posts, decision-maker summary, action register and Conductor governance trail.

## Acceptance criteria
- Red-team risks are mapped to concrete changes and not merely listed.
- The public thesis is modest, testable and not overclaimed.
- The current reform pathway is the main comparator.
- "Uncapped" is consistently paired with scheduled, rules-based, audited, clinically governed and place-accountable.
- RACMA remains excluded from active outreach.
- The model card clearly prevents predictive/fiscal overclaiming.
- First six Substack posts include the revised caveats and fair-counterargument framing.
- Conductor track, README, VERSION and release notes are updated.
