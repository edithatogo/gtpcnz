# Spec — v1.7.1 model hardening and launch simplification

## Goal
Harden the v1.7.0 parameterised scaffold and simplify the public launch pathway.

## Requirements
- Tier the 70-parameter scaffold into core, extended and exploratory parameters.
- Create a parameter identifiability map.
- Create a model card and figure-caption audit.
- Add source-confidence labels and a falsification table.
- Undertake red-team and devil's advocate reviews.
- Remove RACMA from active outreach; leave as passive re-entry only.
- Prepare first-six-post launch copies with standard disclaimer and source labels.
- Add stakeholder validation survey and decision-maker summary.

## Non-goals
- Do not add more conceptual games.
- Do not claim empirical calibration.
- Do not proactively seek RACMA sponsorship.
