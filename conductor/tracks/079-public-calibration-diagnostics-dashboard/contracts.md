# Contracts

- Calibration status must distinguish `passed_public_aggregate_gate`, `readiness_only`, `candidate_source`, `blocked`, and `not_applicable`.
- Every diagnostic must include source/provenance and not-valid-for wording.
- No chart may imply causal, implementation-impact, workforce, hospital-demand, ED-reduction, or precise fiscal validation.
- Blocked evidence lanes must remain visible.
- Dash must not depend on persistent disk, private data, or paid infrastructure.
