# Track 012 spec: uncertainty and calibration readiness

**Status:** Complete

## Objective

Move from one-off game outputs to an auditable uncertainty layer that shows which assumptions drive the results and what data are needed for calibration.

## Scope

Included:

- parameter-prior register;
- Monte Carlo perturbation of scenario levers;
- sensitivity rankings for welfare and hospital pressure;
- validation backlog;
- calibration protocol;
- policy brief on empirical tests.

Excluded:

- real-world calibration;
- causal inference using linked administrative data;
- cost-effectiveness analysis;
- policy endorsement.

## Success criteria

- Every demonstrative parameter has an interpretation and evidence need.
- Uncertainty results are reproducible from code.
- Scenario comparisons are labelled as model-generated indices, not empirical.
- A calibration protocol defines the next research stage.
- Tests pass.
