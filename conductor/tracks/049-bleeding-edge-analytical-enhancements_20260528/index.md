# Bleeding Edge Analytical Enhancements

This track turns the broader recommendations into a concrete backlog. It sits above the equilibrium-solver discussion and focuses on which additional inputs, subgroup views, secondary analyses, visualisations, and simulations are worth adding without breaking the public-data boundary.

## Files

- `spec.md` - enhancement map, candidate features, and decision rules.
- `plan.md` - stepwise work to rank, scope, and verify the enhancements.
- `metadata.json` - track status and output contract.

## Scope

- public-data anchored inputs only;
- subgroup and scenario analysis only at aggregate level;
- no patient-level forecasting claims;
- no calibration upgrade unless separately approved.
