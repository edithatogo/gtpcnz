# Track 017 — Substack-ready publication layer

## Goal

Turn the policy-research package into a human-readable 12-part Substack series with diagrams, source links, glossary, plain-English explainers and Dylan-style narrative.

## Acceptance criteria

- Twelve post drafts exist.
- Each post has a figure reference and at least five unique external links.
- Abbreviations are spelled out in running text or limited to source titles.
- Average sentence length is suitable for a motivated 14-year-old reader.
- A source bank, figure-placement guide, alt-text guide, glossary and publication-readiness report exist.
- The series is candid, systems-minded, non-partisan, careful with uncertainty and not overclaiming predictive validity.
