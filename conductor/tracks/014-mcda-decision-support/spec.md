# Track 014 - Game-informed MCDA decision support v0.9.0

## Objective

Create a deliberative decision-support layer that uses the mapped games to structure MCDA scoring, stakeholder weighting, policy option comparison and validation planning.

## Scope

- Diagnostic game-position scoring.
- Policy-option MCDA scoring.
- Weight-set sensitivity analysis.
- Stakeholder workshop guide.
- CSV templates and executable model.
- MCDA report, brief and workbook.

## Non-goals

- Empirical calibration.
- Formal policy endorsement.
- Replacement of stakeholder deliberation or equity review.
