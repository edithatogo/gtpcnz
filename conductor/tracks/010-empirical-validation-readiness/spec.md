# Track 010 — Empirical validation readiness

## Purpose

Convert the v0.4.1 research audit from a conceptual traceability pack into a practical empirical validation workbench. The track makes the game-theory mapping testable by linking claims to sources, standards, data requirements, stakeholder validation, model parameters and quality gates.

## Scope

Included:

- claim-to-source ledger;
- source registry and evidence grading;
- artefact lineage graph;
- model parameter inventory;
- PRISMA-ScR, STRESS, ODD and CHEERS crosswalks;
- qualitative validation instruments;
- quality gates and decision rules;
- minimum viable simulation plan;
- reproducibility checks.

Excluded:

- completed systematic review;
- calibrated simulation outputs;
- stakeholder interview results;
- final policy endorsement.

## Success criteria

1. Every material policy-game claim has a claim ID.
2. Every claim ID has a source, theory basis, or explicit testing requirement.
3. Every mapped game has at least one proposed empirical test.
4. Every proposed model output has a named data source or data gap.
5. The next-stage review and simulation work can be commenced by collaborators without reconstructing the logic from scratch.
