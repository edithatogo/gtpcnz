    ## Goal

    Specify system dynamics, agent-based and game-theoretic models to test funding architecture hypotheses.

    ## Acceptance criteria

    - The output has a clear thesis.
    - Key claims are source-linked or labelled as hypotheses.
    - Counterarguments and risks are included.
    - Equity, rurality and workforce implications are considered.
    - The work is versioned and cross-linked to related tracks.

    ## Outputs

    - Model overview
- System dynamics spec
- ABM spec
- Game-theoretic spec
- Scenario library
- Validation plan

    ## Dependencies

    - Core thesis document.
    - Evidence anchors from Ministry of Health, Health NZ, NZ HDSR, Australian GP Incentives Review and NZMJ guidance.
    - Stakeholder feedback from RACMA and sector colleagues.
